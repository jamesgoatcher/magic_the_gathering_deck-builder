Magic: The Gathering Deck Builder allows you to query Magic: the Gathering Developers' API for virtually any card produced, which is in excess of 10,000. Additionally, you can create your own virtual deck by registering a profile and saving the cards you fancy.

Magic: The Gathering is a collectable trading card game produced by Wizards of the Coast. Famed for its intricacies and lush artwork, Magic: The Gathering claims over 20 million players worldwide.
